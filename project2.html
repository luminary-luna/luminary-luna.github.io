Walter Mitty

Tool(s): Adobe Illustrator

Project Description: "Walter Mitty" is my original movie poster design for the short story of "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty" which tells the story of a maladaptive daydreamer. The concept inspired me greatly and made me think of the expression "head in the clouds" which I used as the basis of the design. I also took some more minor inspiration from the real movie posters used to promote the live action movie rendition of the very same story.

Project caption: Head in the Clouds.
