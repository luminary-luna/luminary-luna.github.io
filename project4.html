12 Months at Disneyland

Tool(s): Premier Pro

Project Description: “12 Months at Disneyland” is an editing project that complies 12 different clips taken at Disneyland. The decision to base my project on Disneyland of all things was because of its various special festive or holiday events that. The park is even widely decorated for its more prominent holidays such as the Winter Holdiay season and Halloween. I thought it would be interesting to capture how the park’s decorations and scenery changed with each and every month.

Project caption: The happiest place on earth.
