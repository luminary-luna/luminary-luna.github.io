Clear Skies

Tool(s): Adobe Animate

Project Description: “Clear Skies” is an animation project that utilizes motion tweening. As for the concept, I was inspired by my window view from the library where I was able to see planes from the San Jose Airport take off. The overall look was meant to mimic that of a children’s drawing come to life.

Project caption: Early Morning Departure from SJC.
