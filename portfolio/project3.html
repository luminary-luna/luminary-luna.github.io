Sturgery

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: "Sturgery" is my design for a "new species" turning a sturgeon into a...sturgeon. The originally idea comes from a line from the game "Animal Crossing: New Horizons" that makes a pun about a sturgeon performing "sturgery" or surgery like a surgeon. The line makes an appearance about the sturgeon who now adorns surgical attire.

Project caption: "Wonder if it can perform sturgery..."
